Hi there, I'm Michael Hudson, and I'm a software developer currently based out of Newcastle, Australia. I write software for a living, and like to think I'm getting better at it all the time. My current goal is to learn how to manage small software teams in order to build something that I wouldn't be capable of building on my own.

I've been studying Software Engineering at the University of Newcastle for many years, and for most of those I've been working full time as a software developer building banking software. This has given me quite a lot of experience with Java, including JEE, Spring, Maven and a litany of developer tools. Having worked on projects ranging from single developer side projects to multi-year multi-million dollar projects I've gained a wide array of different perspectives on project management and development practices, allowing me to have a somewhat unique insight in to many of the problems I face in both areas.

Outside of work and university studies I spend a lot of my time tinkering with technology, including things like setting up servers, fiddling with build systems, and buying horrendously expensive keyboards.
